# Identifying The Problem

Monosodium Glutamate Or msg for short in food.

MSG is a popular food additive that has been deemed to be unhealthy and dangerous for your health. It has gained a bad reputation to the point where there are even petitions to remove the ingredient in everyday food.

Adding on, certain restaurants would even advertise their food as MSG free in order to attract customers who look to eat healthier. MSG is used to add flavor to a dish.

There has also been cases where people have stated that MSG is causing obesity and high blood pressure.
